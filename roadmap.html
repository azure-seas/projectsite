Phase 1:
Launch the Azure Seas NFT Collection


Objective: Featuring unique anime-style artwork of characters, factions, creatures, treasures, maps, artifacts & more.


Highlights:


  • Introduce the 5 factions: Pirates, Pirate Hunters, Azure Royal Navy, Shadow Council, and Freefolk.
  • Showcase the captivating backstory & lore of the Azure Seas.
  • Offer exclusive NFTs with varying rarities and special attributes.

Phase 2:
Community Engagement and Growth


Objective: Build a vibrant & engaged community around the Azure Seas universe.


Highlights:


  • Host interactive events, AMAs, & giveaways to foster community participation.
  • Create social media channels & a dedicated Discord server for discussions & updates.
  • Launch a community-driven lore expansion initiative, allowing fans to contribute to the Azure Seas story.

Phase 3:
Sell Out and Reward Early Supporters


Objective: Achieve a successful sell-out of the initial NFT collection.


Highlights:


  • Implement a reward system for early supporters and top collectors.
  • Distribute limited-edition NFTs and exclusive perks to loyal community members.
  • Begin planning for future NFT drops and expansions based on community feedback.

Phase 4:
Develop the Azure Seas RPG Game


Objective: Transition from NFT collection to developing a fully immersive RPG game.


Highlights:


  • Assemble a talented development team to bring the Azure Seas world to life.

  • Begin pre-production, including game design, storyboarding, and concept art.
  • Involve the community in the development process through surveys, beta testing, and sneak peeks.
